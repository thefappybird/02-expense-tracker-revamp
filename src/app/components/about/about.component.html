Trackr helps you easily manage your money. Track your spending, set budgets, and see where your money goes with simple charts and summaries. No complicated steps—just a clean, easy-to-use tool to keep your finances organized.

Why Use It? Because money management should be stress-free. Whether you’re saving for a goal or just want to control daily spending, this app gives you a clear picture of your finances. Quickly add expenses, check budgets, and even convert currencies—all in one place. Stay on top of your money without the hassle.
